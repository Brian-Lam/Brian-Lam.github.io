About Me

My name is Brian Lam (but you probably knew that already). I was born and raised in New York City, and graduated from Stuyvesant High School in 2013.

I'm currently pursuing a dual degree in Computer Science and Computer Engineering at Washington University in St. Louis. At school, I also work as a tutor for Data Structures and Algorithms and as a TA for Rapid Prototype Development. After having spent time in school and having tutored over 30 students, I've really grown to love teaching and helping others.

I love reading about topics that I'm interested in, like Machine Learning, Data Analysis, and Web Security. In my free time, I also enjoy cooking, running, and learning to play the guitar.
