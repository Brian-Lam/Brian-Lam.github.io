About Me

Some tidbits about me (since you're stalking my site already)

My name is Brian Lam - I was born and raised in New York City, and graduated from Stuyvesant High School in 2013. I'm currently pursuing a dual degree in Computer Science and Computer Engineering at Washington University in St. Louis.

At school, I also work as a tutor for Data Structures and Algorithms and as a TA for Rapid Prototype Development and Creative Programming. After having spent time in school and having tutored over 30 students, I've really grown to enjoy teaching and helping others.

Above all else, I am a developer. I love working with other people to bring a project to life and create something beautiful. There's always something to learn and a great idea waiting to be built.

I love reading about topics that I'm interested in, like Machine Learning, Data Analysis, and Web Security. In my free time, I also enjoy cooking, running, and learning to play the guitar.
